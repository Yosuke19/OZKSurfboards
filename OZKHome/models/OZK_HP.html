OZK HP

The HP is our purebred high-performance shortboard, designed for surfers who demand speed, precision, and vertical power. With a modern outline, tuned rocker, and responsive rails, the HP thrives in critical sections and delivers the control needed for progressive maneuvers. From beach breaks to reef setups, this board is built to perform at the highest level.

Key features:

• Modern high-performance shape — refined proportions, sharp rails, and tuned rocker for speed and agility.
• Thruster setup — maximum control and drive for vertical surfing and powerful maneuvers.
• Competition ready — built for progressive surfing, ideal for competitive surfers chasing precision and responsiveness.
• All-round versatility — equally at home in punchy beach breaks or clean reef walls.

The HP model is the go-to choice for surfers pushing performance — fast, powerful, and made for taking your surfing to the next level.
