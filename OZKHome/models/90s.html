90's

The 90’s is a throwback with a twist — a board that channels the iconic 90’s vibe with its sleeker nose and refined proportions, while blending in modern design features for performance where it counts. With updated rails and a tuned bottom contour, this thruster setup brings speed, control, and precision into the modern era.

Key features:

• Retro-inspired outline — narrow nose and classic proportions for that true 90’s feel.
• Modern performance core — updated center-to-bottom design keeps it fast, drivey, and sharp under your feet.
• Thruster setup — maximum control for vertical maneuvers and snappy responsiveness.
• High-performance versatility — excels in fast, steep waves and rewards aggressive surfing.

The 90’s model is built for surfers chasing that nostalgic look, with the responsiveness and speed of today’s high-performance shortboard design.
