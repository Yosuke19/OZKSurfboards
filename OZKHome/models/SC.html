SC

The SC is our modern take on the classic fish, blending speed, flow, and high-performance responsiveness into one versatile shape. Designed for surfers who want the glide and drive of a fish with the precision of a shortboard, the SC is built to light up everything from playful beach breaks to powerful reef setups.

Key features:

• Fish DNA, high-performance feel — wider outline for speed and paddling ease, paired with refined rails and rocker for sharp, responsive turns.
• Fast & fun — down-the-line speed with effortless flow, perfect for linking carves or blasting sections.
• 5-fin versatility — standard 5-fin box setup lets you ride it as a thruster for tighter control or as a quad for maximum drive and release.
• All-round adaptability — excels in everyday conditions, from waist-high beach breaks to overhead reef surf.

The SC is the go-to all-rounder for surfers chasing a board that’s fun, fast, and equally at home in small playful surf as it is in punchy, powerful waves.
