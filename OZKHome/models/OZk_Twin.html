OZK Twin

The Twin Pin sits right between our high-performance designs and the La Jolla twin, blending speed, style, and aggression in one versatile shape. With its refined outline and twin-fin setup, this board brings stylish flow while still allowing for sharp, powerful maneuvers. Add channels for extra hold and grip, and the Twin Pin becomes a truly dynamic twin for surfers who want performance without losing style.

Key features:

• Twin pin outline — sleek, stylish look with drive and flow.
• Between HP & La Jolla — combines high-performance responsiveness with smooth twin-fin style.
• Aggressive yet fluid — capable of powerful maneuvers while keeping classic twin speed.
• Optional channels — extra grip and control when the waves get steeper.

The Twin Pin is built for surfers who want the freedom of a twin with added bite and aggression — a board that looks stylish, feels fast, and performs in critical sections.
