OZK Mid

The OZK Mid is all about timeless glide with a modern touch. A mid-length designed for smooth cruising yet still responsive underfoot, it’s the perfect balance between flow and control. With a versatile fin setup and refined bottom contours, the OZK Mid lets you draw long, stylish lines while keeping plenty of performance in reserve.

Key features:

• Mid-length design — effortless paddle power and glide for connecting sections with style.
• 2+1 or single fin setup — classic single fin flow, or add side bites for a touch of extra control.
• Smooth bottom concave — generates speed and delivers that silky, cruise-like sensation.
• Responsive feel — stable and smooth, yet quick to react when you put it on rail.

The OZK Mid is made for surfers chasing that magnificent glide — smooth, stylish, and endlessly fun across all conditions.
